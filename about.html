About Wendy

San Jose physical therapist specializing in recovery planning, lymphatic care, and return-to-fitness guidance.

Wendy Noakes, PT, DPT, SCS, CSCS is a physical therapist serving clients in San Jose. Her work blends ACOLS-trained manual lymphatic drainage, rehab principles, and fitness planning for people preparing for or recovering from procedures.

The practice is designed for clients who want more than a single treatment: a thoughtful plan for swelling support, mobility, activity progression, and a confident return to movement.

Care approach

What clients can expect

Clear intake

Before your first session, Wendy reviews goals, relevant history, procedure timing, current fitness level, and precautions.

Gentle pacing

Hands-on care and exercise guidance are matched to your stage of healing, comfort, breath, positioning, and provider restrictions.

Practical education

You leave with clear next steps for activity, recovery pacing, and when to contact your medical provider.
