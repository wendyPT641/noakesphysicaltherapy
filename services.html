Services

Manual lymphatic drainage, recovery PT, and fitness planning in San Jose.

Sessions begin with a short intake and are adapted to your goals, procedure timeline, activity level, and provider restrictions. Wendy’s background as a Doctor of Physical Therapy, Sports Certified Specialist, Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist, and ACOLS-trained MLD provider helps bridge hands-on lymphatic care with practical movement planning.

01

Post-procedure recovery support in San Jose

Post-procedure sessions are intended for clients who have been cleared for gentle hands-on care and guided movement. Wendy can combine manual lymphatic drainage in San Jose with mobility education, positioning strategies, walking progression, and a staged return-to-activity plan.

  • Gentle, light-pressure lymphatic techniques
  • Positioning, breathing, and mobility adapted to your comfort
  • Education on pacing, activity progression, and warning signs to discuss with your provider
  • Return-to-movement guidance that respects your healing timeline

02

Pre-procedure fitness planning for stronger recovery foundations

Pre-procedure planning helps clients build a stronger foundation before an approved procedure. Depending on your timeline and goals, your plan may focus on mobility, strength, walking tolerance, breathing mechanics, posture, and habits that support a smoother recovery period.

  • Baseline movement and fitness screen
  • Simple home plan for strength, mobility, and walking
  • Education on what to maintain, pause, or modify around procedure timing
  • Planning for the first safe steps back into activity

03

Return-to-fitness coaching

Once you are cleared to progress, Wendy can help you rebuild gradually. Plans can bridge the gap between “I’m allowed to exercise” and “I know exactly how to restart without doing too much too soon.”

  • Progressive walking, mobility, and strength plans
  • Modifications for swelling, fatigue, sensitivity, or movement restrictions
  • Guidance for returning to gym workouts, Pilates, yoga, or daily activity
  • Check-ins to adjust the plan as your tolerance improves

FAQ

Common questions

Is this just manual lymphatic drainage?

No. MLD can be part of care, but Wendy can also provide physical therapist-led movement, rehab, and fitness planning for the pre- and post-procedure period.

When can I book after surgery?

Follow your surgeon’s instructions. If you have fever, infection, unusual pain, suspected clotting, or a new medical concern, check with your medical provider before booking.

Can I start before my procedure?

Yes. Pre-procedure planning can help you build strength, mobility, walking tolerance, and realistic expectations before your procedure, as long as the plan fits your provider’s guidance.

Do you treat medical lymphedema?

This website is currently written for pre- and post-procedure recovery optimization and wellness-focused clients. A medical lymphedema care pathway can be added later if you want to market that service specifically.

Where can I read more about MLD?

Helpful public resources include Cleveland Clinic and Cancer Research UK.
