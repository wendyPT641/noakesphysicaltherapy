Why physical therapist-led care

More than a single session. A plan for the whole recovery arc.

Manual lymphatic drainage uses light, slow, rhythmic techniques intended to encourage lymphatic flow and support fluid movement. Wendy pairs that gentle hands-on care with practical rehab and fitness planning so clients understand what to do before, between, and after visits.

Your plan can include mobility work, breathing, gentle activation, walking progression, scar-sensitive movement education, and return-to-exercise guidance within your provider’s restrictions.
